Fluffy French Toast

Description

This is the best French toast recipe. It's different because it uses flour. I have given it to some friends and they've all liked it better than the French toast they usually make! View the Full Recipe.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Directions

Step 1

Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.

Step 2

Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.

Step 3

Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.

Step 4

Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
